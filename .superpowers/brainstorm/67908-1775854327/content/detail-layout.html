Tap entry → navigate to a new screen. Standard iOS pattern. Back button returns to timeline. Reactions + flag button inline.

B — Bottom sheet

Tap entry → sheet slides up over timeline. Swipe down to dismiss. Context stays visible behind sheet. Quicker to get back to timeline.
